## Vesting

Recommended investor-standard structure:

- Four-year vesting.
- One-year cliff.
- Monthly vesting thereafter.
- Unvested equity returns to the company on departure.
- Acceleration only if negotiated.

## IP Assignment

Each founder assigns to the company all rights, title, and interest in:

- Source code.
- Designs.
- Product names.
- Documentation.
- Data schemas.
- Threat intelligence datasets created for the company.
- Domain names and brand assets.
- Accounts created for the business.
- Inventions, trade secrets, and know-how related to Noytrix.

## Confidentiality

Founders must protect:

- Source code.
- Threat intelligence sources and data.
- Credentials and secrets.
- User data.
- Commercial plans.
- Investor materials.

## Decision-Making

Major decisions requiring written approval:

- Issuing equity.
- Taking investment.
- Selling company assets.
- Hiring executives.
- Taking debt.
- Changing product direction materially.
- Assigning or licensing core IP.

## Departures

If a founder leaves:

- Unvested equity is forfeited.
- Company retains all assigned IP.
- Confidentiality continues.
- Company accounts, repositories, domains, and credentials are returned.
- Any consulting or advisory role must be separately documented.

- Mediation.
