# Company Documents Checklist

Confidential legal readiness checklist.

## Formation

| Document | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Certificate / articles of incorporation | To collect | Required for investor due diligence |
| Company bylaws / operating agreement | To collect | Required |
| Founder equity issuance documents | To collect | Must match cap table |
| Board / member consent records | To collect | Include financing approvals |
| Registered address and agent records | To collect | Jurisdiction-specific |

## Tax and Banking

| Document | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tax registration number | To collect | EIN or local equivalent |
| Bank account confirmation | To collect | No credentials |
| Accounting records | To prepare | Monthly P&L and balance sheet |
| Payment processor records | To prepare | Google Play, RevenueCat, Stripe if used |

## Product and Compliance

| Document | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Terms of Service | To prepare | Must cover no investment advice |
| Privacy Policy | To prepare | Must match Google Play Data Safety |
| Data Processing Addendum | Future | Needed for B2B |
| Security policy | Draft | See security overview |
| Incident response policy | Draft | See security overview |
| Refund policy | To prepare | Align with app store and subscriptions |

## Intellectual Property

| Document | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Founder IP assignment | Required | Must assign code, product, brand, designs |
| Contractor IP assignments | Required if contractors used | Must include work product and moral rights waiver where lawful |
| Open-source dependency review | To prepare | Check license compatibility |
| Trademark search | To prepare | Noytrix brand |
| Domain ownership proof | To collect | Registrar screenshots or invoices |

## Investor-Ready Standard

No investor should receive:

- Raw `.env` files.
- Private keys.
- Database dumps containing user data.
- Passwords.
- Service account JSON files.
- Keystore files.
- Unredacted personal identity documents.
