## Burn Rate

```text
Gross burn = total monthly cash expenses
Net burn = gross burn - cash collected revenue
Runway = cash balance / net burn
```

## Financial Hygiene

Investor-grade reporting should separate:

- Production revenue.
- Refunded revenue.
- Sandbox transactions.
- Internal/test purchases.
- Manual promotional entitlements.
- One-time setup costs.
- Recurring cloud costs.
