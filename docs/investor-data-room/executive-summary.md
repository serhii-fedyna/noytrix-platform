# Noytrix Executive Summary

Confidential draft.

## Company

Noytrix is a crypto risk intelligence platform designed to help users, wallets, communities, and Web3 platforms understand risk before opening a link, connecting a wallet, signing a transaction, approving a contract, or buying a token.

## Product

Noytrix currently includes:

- Mobile application.
- Backend risk intelligence engine.
- Public web experience.
- Browser extension codebase.
- Telegram bot codebase.
- API and billing infrastructure.
- Product analytics and internal dashboard.
- Threat intelligence, graph, reputation, and AI explanation layers.

## Why It Matters

Crypto scams are no longer simple spam links. Modern attacks combine impersonation, fake airdrops, wallet drainers, malicious approvals, obfuscated JavaScript, fake support, suspicious contracts, and cross-platform social engineering.

Users need a product that translates technical risk into a clear human answer.

## Differentiation

Noytrix is not only a wrapper around third-party APIs. The platform is moving toward first-party intelligence:

- Internal verdict engine.
- Own scam database.
- Source reputation.
- Graph-based risk propagation.
- Scam family classification.
- Runtime Web3 analysis.
- AI explanation connected to backend evidence.

## Business Model

Noytrix can monetize through:

- Consumer subscription.
- API access.
- B2B intelligence feed.
- Enterprise dashboard.
- Community and wallet integrations.
