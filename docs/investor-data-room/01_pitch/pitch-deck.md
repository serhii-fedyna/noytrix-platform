# Noytrix Investor Pitch Deck

Confidential draft. Prepared for investor discussion.

## Slide 1 - One-Line Vision

**Noytrix** turns crypto risk into a clear human answer before the user clicks, connects a wallet, signs, approves, buys, or trusts a link.

## Slide 2 - The Pain

Crypto users do not lose money because they lack more dashboards. They lose money because the decision happens in seconds, under pressure, with warnings they do not understand.

- Fake support pages look legitimate.
- Wallet drainers hide behind normal-looking approvals.
- Scam tokens and fake airdrops move faster than static lists.
- Users need a plain answer: "is this dangerous, why, and what should I do?"

## Slide 3 - Why The Market Is Big

Crypto is no longer a niche.

- Crypto.com estimated **741M global crypto owners in 2025**.
- a16z reported crypto market capitalization crossed **$4T in 2025** and mobile wallet users reached all-time highs.
- FBI IC3 reported Americans lost **more than $11B** in cryptocurrency-related complaints in 2025.
- Chainalysis estimated crypto scams and fraud could exceed **$17B** for 2025 as more illicit addresses are identified.

## Slide 4 - Why Now

The market is moving from simple trading to always-on onchain activity: wallets, stablecoins, tokenized assets, Telegram communities, airdrops, DEXs, signatures, and app-to-wallet flows.

Every extra wallet action creates a new security moment. The product that wins is the one that explains risk before the loss.

## Slide 5 - The Solution

Noytrix is a crypto risk intelligence layer across:

- mobile app;
- browser extension;
- website and API;
- Telegram bot;
- backend verdict engine.

The user sees one clear answer in their selected language: what is risky, why it matters, what could happen, and what to do next.

## Slide 6 - Product Surface

Noytrix checks:

- URLs and domains;
- wallets and addresses;
- tokens and contracts;
- transactions and signatures;
- airdrops and drainer pages;
- market and security events.

## Slide 7 - What Already Works

Current foundation:

- production backend;

## Slide 8 - Technology Advantage

Noytrix is moving from "API wrapper" to first-party intelligence:

- internal verdict engine;
- Noytrix Scam Database;
- source reputation and confidence scoring;
- entity reputation with time decay;
- graph risk propagation;
- scam campaign clustering;
- runtime approve/sign analysis;
- AI explanation in plain language.

## Slide 9 - Competitive Landscape

Competitors prove demand, but leave room for a user-first layer:

| Company / Product | Strength | Gap Noytrix Targets |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blockaid | Deep B2B wallet and transaction security | Primarily enterprise/wallet infrastructure, not consumer-first plain-language product |
| Chainabuse | Large scam reporting network | Reactive reporting; not a full runtime consumer experience |
| Web3 Antivirus | Browser extension and transaction warnings | Narrower surface; less platform-wide mobile/API/community layer |
| Wallet-native warnings | Distribution at signing moment | Often technical, limited explanation, limited cross-product intelligence |

## Slide 10 - Why Noytrix Can Win

Noytrix can win by combining five things in one product:

1. Consumer mobile experience.
2. Runtime browser protection.
3. API and B2B intelligence path.
4. Own intelligence database and graph.
5. Human AI explanations in the user's language.

The wedge is simple: users understand Noytrix faster than they understand raw security data.
